Meet Joanne

Joanne LaSpina

Certified Educational Planner

Experience

  Joanne's clients benefit from her 20-plus years of professional experience in higher education, counseling and writing. She regularly participates in committee work, conferences, forums, and discussions to stay informed of changing trends. Joanne has visited hundreds of campuses and shares her knowledge of colleges to help match students with right-fit opportunities.

Education

  Joanne received her undergraduate degree in psychology from Millersville University and her graduate degree in counseling psychology from Arcadia University. Since then, Joanne has completed all coursework for a certificate for Independent Educational Consultants in the University of California Irvine program.

Accomplishments

  Joanne has published over 1000 posts, print and online articles, and essays. She has worked with clients in a variety of settings- colleges, schools, workplace, groups, and workshops. Her strong organizational skills, ability to listen and observe, and keen attention to detail, help guide students and families through decisions and actions.
