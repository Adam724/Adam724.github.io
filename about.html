Meet Joanne

Joanne LaSpina

Certified Educational Planner

Experience

  Joanne is a Certified Educational Professional (CEP). All families seeking the services of a college counselor should seek out a CEP, which is the highest certification available in the field. Joanne's clients benefit from her 20-plus years of professional experience in higher education, counseling, and writing. She regularly participates in committee work, conferences, forums, and discussions to stay informed of changing trends. Joanne has visited hundreds of campuses and shares her knowledge of colleges to help match students with right-fit opportunities.

Education

  Joanne received her undergraduate degree in psychology and her graduate degree in counseling psychology. She has completed all coursework for a certificate for Independent Educational Consultants in the University of California Irvine program.

Accomplishments

  Joanne has published over 1000 posts, print and online articles, and essays. She has worked with clients in a variety of settings - colleges, schools, workplaces, groups, and workshops. Her strong organizational skills, ability to listen and observe, and keen attention to detail, help guide students and families through decisions and actions.
